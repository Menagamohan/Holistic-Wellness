1. Homoeopathy

Homoeopathy is a natural system of medicine developed by Samuel Hahnemann. It is based on the principle "like cures like". Small doses of natural substances are used to stimulate the body's healing ability.

Types of Homoeopathy Preparations

  • Mother Tincture (Q)
  • Dilutions (6C, 30C, 200C)
  • Tablets / Globules
  • Triturations
  • Ointments / Drops

2. Ayurveda

Ayurveda is one of the oldest medical systems in India. It focuses on balancing the body’s three doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha using herbs, diet, and healthy lifestyle practices.

Types of Ayurvedic Medicines

  • Churna – Herbal powders
  • Vati / Gutika – Tablets
  • Kashaya / Kwath – Decoctions
  • Asava & Arishta – Fermented liquids
  • Avaleha / Lehyam – Herbal pastes
  • Ghrita – Medicated ghee
  • Taila – Medicated oils
  • Bhasma – Mineral preparations
  • Rasayana – Rejuvenation
  • Arka – Herbal distillates
  • Lepa – Herbal paste
  • Guggulu preparations

3. Yoga & Naturopathy

Yoga and Naturopathy focus on natural healing methods and prevention. These systems use natural therapies such as yoga exercises, diet therapy, herbal medicine, and natural elements.

Therapies & approaches

  • Herbal Medicine
  • Diet & Nutrition therapy
  • Mud Therapy
  • Hydrotherapy
  • Massage & Acupuncture
  • Yoga asanas & Pranayama
  • Fasting & detox

4. Unani

Unani medicine originated from ancient Greek medicine and was further developed by Arab and Persian physicians. It focuses on balancing four body humors to maintain health.

Types of Unani Medicines

  • Safoof (Powder)
  • Habb / Qurs (Tablet)
  • Sharbat (Syrup)
  • Arq (Distillate)
  • Majoon (Semi-solid paste)
  • Jawarish (Digestive paste)
  • Khamira (Electuary)
  • Roghan (Medicated oil)
  • Marham (Ointment)
  • Laooq (Thick syrup)

5. Siddha

Siddha medicine is an ancient system of medicine from South India. It uses herbs, minerals, and metals to treat diseases and promote health and longevity.

Types of Siddha Medicines

  • Chooranam (Powder)
  • Kudineer (Herbal decoction)
  • Kashayam (Herbal extract)
  • Mathirai (Tablet)
  • Parpam (Calx)
  • Chenduram (Red oxide)
  • Mezhugu (Wax medicine)
  • Legiyam / Ilagam (Paste)
  • Thailam (Medicated oil)
  • Kuzhambu (Liquid prep)
