differences at a glance

Homoeopathy

  • Origin: Germany (18th c.)
  • Core forms: Mother tincture, dilutions (6C‑200C), triturations
  • Unique: Potentisation, globules, minimum dose
  • No raw herb use ultra‑dilute

Ayurveda

  • Origin: India (3000+ years)
  • Core forms: Churna, vati, kashaya, asava/arishta, ghrita, taila, bhasma
  • Unique: Bhasma (calcined metals), guggulu, lehyam
  • Dosha‑based holistic

Yoga & Naturopathy

  • Origin: Global / India (yoga)
  • Core forms: Herbal medicine, supplements, mud therapy
  • Unique: Acupuncture needles, yoga mat, natural mud
  • Non‑invasive & lifestyle drugless

Unani

  • Origin: Greece / Persia (Greco‑Arab)
  • Core forms: Safoof, habb/qurs, sharbat, arq, majoon, jawarish, khamira
  • Unique: Semi‑solid pastes (majoon, jawarish), khamira, laooq
  • Humoural theory temperament

Siddha

  • Origin: South India (Tamil)
  • Core forms: Chooranam, kudineer, mathirai, parpam, chenduram, mezhugu
  • Unique: Parpam (calx), chenduram (red oxide), mezhugu (wax form)
  • Alchemy & muppu metal‑based
